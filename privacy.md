Privacy Policy – PaperEdit

PaperEdit is a macOS application designed to work entirely on your computer.

Data Collection

PaperEdit does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information.

The app:

does not require an account

does not track usage

does not use analytics

does not connect to external servers

Files

All files you open, import, or create remain on your Mac.
When you import images or script PDFs, they are copied only into your project folder and are never uploaded anywhere.

Network Access

PaperEdit does not communicate with the internet. The app functions completely offline.
